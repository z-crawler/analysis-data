Job Description


Works as part of the Enterprise Infrastructure team to apply information systems technology solutions in support of identified business problems. Participates in the evaluation, selection, implementation and on-going support of software, hardware and tools used by the Enterprise Infrastructure team. Works under minimal supervision

  1. Works with a variety of outsourced production support and application development teams to ensure that technical solutions meet overall business information requirements and application's functional requirements.

  2. Assists branch offices and internal customers with all aspects of software for the mid-range computer systems. Troubleshoots software problems. Recommends corrective/adaptive changes.

  3. Sets up/audits the user security profiles and prepares security audit reports. Ensures the integrity of operating systems and prepares annual audits and audit reviews.

  4. Develops and executes plans for implementation of new tools and/or technology. Conducts plan reviews and walkthroughs in an effort to communicate plans to other IS groups and to ensure the coordination and coorperation of all parties involved.

  5. Writes technical documentation for existing hardware and software systems. Evaluates current/future needs, identifies existing/potential problems, and recommends modifications to improve processing capabilities.

  6. Monitors remote and local systems performance to identify problems. Modifies system configurations to improve processing and availability as needed.

  7. Identifies, obtains from vendors, and applies temporary fixes or upgrades to operating system software as needed. Serves as primary contact with vendors during problem resolution and plans and performs periodic upgrades to new releases.

  8. Ensures that work requests and reported problems are handled in a professional, organized and timely manner. Provides management with periodic formal and informal reports on the status of projects and problems.

  9. Evaluates/tests new system management products and recommends their use by the company as appropriate.

Job Requirements


To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodation may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
  • Installation/Administration of Red Hat Enterprise Linux or Oracle Enterprise Linux

  • Installation/Administration of JBoss

  • Administration of EMC SAN (Clariion)

  • Administration of EMC DataDomain

  • Administration of VMWare vSphere 4.x or 5.x

  • Administration of vRanger

  • Familiarity with IBM xSeries and Cisco UCS hardware a plus

  • Familiarity with Compuware Vantage a plus



Required Experience


High school diploma and technical training or the equivalent plus a minimum of five years of Linux operating systems experience generally required. Strong knowledge of operating system software and related installation/maintenance requirements, and hardware configurations required. Excellent communications skills required.
