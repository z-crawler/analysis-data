Job Description

Acts and the primary technical resource for the RFP bid process.  Responsible for actively driving and managing the technology evaluation stage of the bid process, working in conjunction with the sales team as the key technical advisor and product advocate for our products.  Articulates technology and product positioning to both business and technical users. Identifies all technical issues of assigned contracts to ensure complete customer satisfaction through all stages of the process.  Establishes and maintains strong relationships throughout the bid cycle and beyond.

  • Drive value creation approach to customers to maximize business pipeline from existing customers and new prospective customers following the internal sales process and proposal process.
  • Technical discovery conversations, business discovery conversations, solution demonstrations to both technical and executive audiences, custom demonstration creation, proof of concept delivery, RFI and RFP responses, and communication with post-sales implementation resources.
  • Develop strong relationship and trust with client or potential client to achieve solutions strategies and objectives.
  • Utilizing the knowledge of the SPC solution. Deliver sales messaging, competitive solution differentiation, and business value messaging.
  • Delivering presentations that are compelling, relevant, simple, appealing, and memorable for each particular audience.
  • Supporting knowledge sharing and revenue pipeline development activities.
  • Validation of sales messaging.
  • Work as liaison between the field and IT; participate in internal community of practice groups, mentor peers, and support one-to-many demand generation activities, industry conferences, and web marketing demonstrations.
  • Support other marketing activities such as cultivating customer references.
  • Assist in completion of special projects and initiatives on an as needed basis.



Job Requirements

Education: Undergraduate degree in Business, Marketing, IT discipline and/or related field.

Experience: 10+ years of experience in sales/support and/or technical consulting with significant exposure to clients across a variety of industries.

Ability to determine a client's business requirements and whether the products being considered are suitable.

Experience and knowledge sizing, installing, configuring and tuning servers, storage, virtual machines.

Knowledge of ITSM, management and monitoring tools.

Ability to stand up new revenue-generating solutions to meet strategic business objectives for clients.

Expertise solving customer ‘points of pain.'

Ability to travel up to 40% to correctional facilities, regional offices, etc.
